Our Responsibility

Melt Enterprise is committed to operating in a socially responsible manner as a global corporate citizen.

Melt follows corporate governance standards that promote integrity, transparency, and accountability in order to protect and enhance Melt Enterprise’s long-term success.

We guide many of the world’s largest multinational corporations and best-known brands to improve the social, ethical, and environmental consequences of their products, services, and supply chains.

We believe local managers are best placed to understand and react to their social business environment. Our integrity is crucial to our business. Melt Enterprise is committed to high standards of professional conduct and to the ethical and fair business dealings with our employees, stakeholders, and third parties.

Our Compliance Code and compliance structure support adherence to our Code of Ethics.
