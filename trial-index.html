About Us

Melt Enterprise is a DGFT approved Pre-shipment Inspection Agency (PSIA). We are authorised to provide Pre-shipment inspection Certificate (PSIC) for metal and paper scrap loading in containers, at the loading port. We ensure that the metal and paper scrap exported to india are free from any hazardous material. The company is authorized to provide Pre-shipment Inspection Certification in 60+ countries.
